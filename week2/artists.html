Shawn Mendes

Biography

Shawn Peter Raul Mendes, born August 8, 1998, in Toronto, Canada, is a globally acclaimed singer-songwriter and musician. Raised in Pickering, Ontario, he began learning guitar at age 14 through online tutorials and soon started posting song covers on Vine, where his talent quickly attracted millions of viewers. In 2014, he signed with Island Records and released his debut EP, followed by his first studio album Handwritten in 2015, which topped the Billboard 200. Known for chart-topping singles like “Stitches,” “Treat You Better,” and “Señorita,” Mendes has earned numerous awards and recognition, including being named one of Time’s 100 most influential people in 2018.
